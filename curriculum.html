Our Curriculum

Our curriculum offers these core subjects: 2 languages(IsiZulu and English), Life Orientation, Mathematics and Physical Sciences. Our choice subjects include : Life Sciences, CAT or I.T, Accounting and Geography. You can only choose 2 from our choice subjects.

Accounting

Accounting is the process of recording financial transactions pertaining to a business. The accounting process includes summarizing, analyzing and reporting these transactions to oversight agencies, regulators and tax collection entities.

Computer Applications Technology

CAT teaches students about the various components involved in computer systems, as well as an introduction into how to use computers effectively in every day situations.

Life Sciences

Life sciences is the study of living organisms. It deals with every aspect of living organisms, from the biology of cells, to the biology of individual organisms, to how these organisms interact with other organisms and their environment.
